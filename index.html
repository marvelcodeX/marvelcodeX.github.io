I'm an information science and engineering student who enjoys building things that feel both intelligent and deeply human. Hackathons taught me how to turn rough ideas into working systems quickly, and projects like Manasvi reminded me that technology can do more than solve tasks — it can create space for comfort, clarity, and care.
